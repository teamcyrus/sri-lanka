Welcome to Sri Lanka

A country more than just bustling cities, a land which possess a proud history to brag about and also a motherland of people who are well known for their hospitality.

  • Sri Lanka is located in the Indian Ocean. Sri Lanka was nicknamed as “The Pearl of the Indian Ocean” considering its shape and the value.
  • The country is officially known as the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
  • As of 2022, population of Sri Lanka is 22,181,000.
  • Sri Lanka spreads in an area of 65,610 km².
  • The capital city of Sri Lanka is Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte while Colombo acts as the executive.
  • Colombo is also considered the largest city in Sri Lanka.
  • Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR) is the currency used in Sri Lanka.
  • In view of economy, Sri Lanka is still a developing country.
  • Sri Lanka is home to many ethnicities and religions which is the reason for Sri Lanka to be rich in cultural diversity.
