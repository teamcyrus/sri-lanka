The Independent's list of the best destinations to travel to in 2023 mentions Sri Lanka as one of the tourist hotspots this year.


The amazing bio diversity of Sri Lanka is the key factor which has resulted in such high attractions from tourists.





Sri Lanka is considered to have the highest amphibian species density in the world with a high concentration in the Sinharaja Rainforest. Meanwhile the whales in the ocean around Sri Lanka have made it one of the best locations for whale watching. The adventurous activities available in the sites are also contributing in claiming tourist attraction. Mirissa is the best place for that. Other than taking a dip in the oceans or snorkelling, scuba diving and surfing are the most popular beach sports in the country.
